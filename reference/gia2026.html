About me

The editor behind the cut.

I'm Geferlene — most people call me Gia. I've spent the last 3+ years cutting videos for YouTube channels, ad campaigns and social media brands across the US, Australia and the Philippines.

I started with a BS in Psychology, which honestly helps more than you'd think — understanding what keeps people watching is half the job. The other half is just caring enough to get every cut right.

When I'm not in Premiere Pro, I'm researching trends, studying what makes great content perform, and figuring out how to make the next edit better than the last.

FAQ

Common questions.

Anything else? Just email me — I reply within 24 hours.

What's your usual turnaround time?

Most edits are delivered within 24–48 hours for short-form content, and 3–5 days for long-form YouTube videos. Rush delivery is available on request.

How many revision rounds are included?

2 rounds of revisions are included in all packages. Additional rounds can be added. I use timestamped video feedback tools to make the process fast and clear.

What software do you use?

Primarily Adobe Premiere Pro and After Effects for professional edits, CapCut for quick short-form content, and Photoshop / Canva for thumbnails and graphics.

What formats do you deliver in?

Any format you need — MP4 (H.264/H.265), MOV, or platform-optimized presets for YouTube, Instagram Reels, TikTok, and Facebook. Just let me know where it's going.

Do you handle YouTube channel management too?

Yes — I have 2+ years of experience managing multiple YouTube channels end to end, including uploading, scheduling, thumbnails, and basic SEO setup.

Are you available for long-term retainer work?

Absolutely. Most of my clients are on monthly arrangements. Retainers get priority turnaround, consistent communication, and a locked rate.
